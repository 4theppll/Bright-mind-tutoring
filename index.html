About Bright Minds Tutoring

Hi! I'm Salahdiin Ahmed Adan, a high school student who knows what it's like to face challenges in school. That's why I started Bright Minds Tutoring - to give back and help students like you understand schoolwork, boost confidence, and succeed in class.

"Every student is a bright mind - they just need support to shine."

Struggling in school? We're here to help - for free. At Bright Minds Tutoring, we offer free one-on-one tutoring for middle schoolers who need extra help, especially those who can't afford paid tutors.
